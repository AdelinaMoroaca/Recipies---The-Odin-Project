Sangria

Sangria is an alcoholic punch that originated in Spain and Portugal ~ it will last for about three to five days in the refrigerator if stored in an airtight container.

Preparation time

  • Total:
  • Preparation:
  • Cooking

Ingredients

  • 1 (750 milliliter) bottle dry red wine
  • ½ cup brandy and triple sec
  • ⅓ cup frozen lemonade concentrate and orange juice
  • ¼ cup lemon juice and white sugar (Optional)
  • 1 medium orange, lemon, lime sliced into rounds
  • 8 maraschino cherries
  • 2 cups carbonated water (Optional)

Instruction

  1. Step 1 :Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2 :Mix together red wine, brandy, triple sec, lemonade concentrate, orange juice, lemon juice, and sugar in a bowl. Add orange, lemon, and lime slices and maraschino cherries.
  3. Step 3 :Serve immediately over ice, or refrigerate 8 hours to overnight for best flavor. For a fizzy sangria, add club soda just before serving.
